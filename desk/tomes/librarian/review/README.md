# Spell of the Librarian's Filter

This scroll contains the sacred and private ritual for reviewing Petitions submitted to the Hall of Voices. It activates the Librarian's Filter, ensuring the integrity and quality of the public chronicle.

**Purpose:** Review contributions to the Library, adjudicating what enters the Alliance's shared knowledge.

**Invocation:** `@librarian/review` or `@librarian/review-petitions`

---

## Operational Guidance

### For the Spirit

When this ritual is invoked:

1. **Read the petition** - Understand what is being submitted
2. **Assess quality** - Does it meet Library standards?
3. **Check resonance** - Does it align with Alliance wisdom?
4. **Verify completeness** - Is it ready for public chronicle?
5. **Recommend action** - Accept, request revision, or decline with reasoning
6. **Execute decision** - Based on Mage's sanction, integrate or return to petitioner
