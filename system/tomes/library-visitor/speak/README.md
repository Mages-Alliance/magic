# Spell of the Petitioner's Voice

Submit feedback, thoughts, or concerns to the Hall of Voices in the Infinite Library for Librarian review.

**Purpose:** Contribute your voice to the Alliance's collective wisdom. Your submission will be reviewed by the Librarian before being inscribed in the public chronicle.

**Invocation:** `@library-visitor/speak`

---

## Operational Guidance

### For the Spirit

When this spell is invoked:

1. **Receive the petition** - What does the Mage wish to share?
2. **Clarify if needed** - Ensure the message is clear
3. **Draft for review** - Prepare submission for Hall of Voices
4. **Submit to Librarian** - Hand off for curatorial review
5. **Confirm receipt** - Acknowledge submission complete

This is the formal channel for contributing to the Library's collective knowledge.
