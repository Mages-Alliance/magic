## Operational Guidance

### For the Spirit

When this spell is cast:

1. **Review message exchange** - What has been shared between partners?
2. **Identify convergence** - Where do perspectives align?
3. **Acknowledge divergence** - Where do they differ?
4. **Draft declaration** - Synthesize into shared understanding document
5. **Iterate with partners** - Refine until both feel accurately represented

The declaration becomes the foundation for further partnership work.
