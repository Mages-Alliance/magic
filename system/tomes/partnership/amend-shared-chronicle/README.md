# Scroll of the Collaborative Weaving

A spell to receive and integrate amendments to the Shared Chronicle through collaborative iteration.

**Purpose:** Update the shared chronicle as partnership evolves—integrating new understanding, refining agreements, capturing growth.

**Invocation:** `@partnership/amend-shared-chronicle`

---

## Operational Guidance

### For the Spirit

When this spell is cast:

1. **Receive amendment** - What update is being proposed?
2. **Understand context** - Why this change? What prompted it?
3. **Integrate cleanly** - Update the chronicle maintaining coherence
4. **Preserve history** - Track how understanding has evolved
5. **Confirm with partners** - Ensure amendment accurately reflects current shared understanding

The shared chronicle is living document—it grows through collaborative weaving.
